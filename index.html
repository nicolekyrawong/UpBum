What we do What is UpBum?

UpBum is a mobile, and Internet-based photo-sharing application and service that allows users to share pictures and videos publicly to followers. It was created by Ronchi Floyd Miong, Nicole Kyra Wong and John Dave Paclar, and launched in March 2018 as a free mobile app exclusively for the Android operating system.
